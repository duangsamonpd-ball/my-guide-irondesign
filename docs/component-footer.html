Product Footer

A site-wide footer promotion. Two variants — Suite showcases the full Iron Suite brand; Default frames a specific product as part of the suite. Each product link is the Product hover sub-component.

      Three structural zones.
      1
      Variant content area — the left headline; changes between Suite (brand logo) and Default (“[Product] is a part of IRONSUITE”).
      2
      CTAs — Tertiary .btn-dark “Get Full 10 products Suite” + Ghost .btn-text-light “Start Free Trial”.
      3
      Product links — the 10-item list, each an instance of the Product hover sub-component.

      When to use
      ◆ Suite
      Use on the homepage and product-agnostic landing pages. Highlights the full 10-product Iron Suite brand — ideal when no single product is the focus.
      ● Default
      Use on individual product pages (IronPDF, IronWord…). The headline names the current product and cross-sells the broader suite.
