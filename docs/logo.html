✳️ Logo

The Iron Software mark is the foundation of every product identity across the suite. This page documents the primary mark, wordmark lockup, color rules, product-specific lockups, and common misuse to avoid.

Clear Space & Minimum Size

Clear space = height of one mark segment (X). Keep this zone free of text, edges, and other logos.

Minimum size
16px — too small
24px — minimum
48px — comfortable
Wordmark Lockup
IRONSOFTWARE

Primary lockup — corporate & cross-product contexts. Icon and wordmark keep fixed relative position and spacing.

Don'ts
✕
IRONSOFTWARE
Don't rearrange elements
Keep the mark and wordmark in fixed relative position — never split or reorder them.
✕
IRONSOFTWARE
Don't recolor the mark
Only use approved brand color tokens — never arbitrary or off-brand hues.
✕
IRONSOFTWARE
Don't add effects
No drop shadows, glows, gradients, or bevels on the mark or wordmark.
✕
IRONSOFTWARE
Don't use busy backgrounds
Avoid photos, patterns, or gradients that fight the mark for attention.
✕
IRONSOFTWARE
Don't use low contrast
Always check contrast against the background — the mark must stay legible.
✕
IRONSOFTWARE
Don't distort proportions
Never stretch or squash the mark — scale it uniformly only.
