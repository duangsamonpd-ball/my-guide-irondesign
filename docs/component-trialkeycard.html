Trial Key Card

The centered, single-field "instant capture" card — one email input, one submit, no label row. Different enough from Form Card (centered icon halo, self-contained input, footnote row) that it's its own component rather than a variant. The card below is live.

Guidelines
✓ Do

Get your free Trial Key instantly.

  • Ask for exactly one thing — the email — and nothing more.
  • Put the value in the bold word; keep the surrounding copy short.
  • Use the notes row to remove doubt ("No credit card").
✗ Don't

Sign up for the trial

  • Don't add extra fields — reach for Form Card when you need them.
  • Don't left-align the input; the card is centered by design.
  • Don't hard-code the submit fill — use --color-primary.
