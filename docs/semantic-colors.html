🎯 Semantic Colors

Colors assigned a purpose — not just a value. Maps each role (Primary, Text, Surface, Feedback) to its palette token so designers and developers always reach for the right color.

🎯

A colour with a job

The Color Palette lists every raw shade (50–950). This page maps those shades to roles — a status, a surface, a border — so you reach for --color-danger, not #E5242A. Click any swatch to copy its token. Every value here is generated from tokens.w3c.json.
